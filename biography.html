Biography | Ben Owino Obiero

Ben Owino Obiero received doctoral degrees in 2011 from the University of Nairobi and an honorary doctorate in 2015 from the University of Nairobi. After postdoctoral years in University of Nairobi, he taught at the same university as part time lecture , before joining Technical University of Kenya in the year 2014, where he is lecture of Pure Mathematics related courses.Since 2017 he is a the head of the department in the school of Matahematics and Acturial Science at The Technical university of Kenya.
